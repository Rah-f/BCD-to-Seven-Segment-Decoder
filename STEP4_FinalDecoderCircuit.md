# Final Decoder Circuit

The final BCD-to-Seven-Segment Decoder circuit is implemented and included in this repository as a Logisim circuit file. It takes 4-bit BCD inputs (A, B, C, D) and generates seven outputs (a–g) to directly drive a seven-segment display.

The circuit is fully functional and can be opened using Logisim or Logisim-evolution for simulation and testing.
